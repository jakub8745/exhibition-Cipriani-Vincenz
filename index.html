  • Thanks for your visit to our site! What you are seeing is our very own 'Metaverse', designed to serve as a state-of-the-art digital archive for a wide portfolio of projects. This immersive 3D archive allows visitors to explore our past and current exhibitions. It is an innovative space that incorporates interactive elements and allows users to engage with digital art, research materials and multimedia content in a new and exciting way. By creating this archive, the BPA aims to increase the accessibility and sustainability of its artistic and research activities, promote global collaboration and enrich the digital humanities.
    The Archive is best viewed on a large screen (desktop computer, laptop). The interior of the gallery can be navigated in a number of ways: with the mouse, the joystick in the bottom left-hand corner of the screen, the arrow or WASD keys, or by double-clicking on the floor and following the blue-yellow icon indicating the direction. You can rotate inwards by holding down the left mouse button and moving the mouse. Double click on the selected image (object) to zoom in, single click again to close the window. You can also click on the name of exhibit on the "Map of the Archive" to be transfer straight to it. We hope you enjoy your visit.
